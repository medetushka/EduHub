Mission & Vision

To be a leading research university that contributes to the sustainable development of Kazakhstan and global society through innovation, education, and research excellence. KazNU aims to be among the top 100 universities worldwide by 2030, fostering international collaboration and producing globally competitive graduates.

History & Achievements

Founded in 1934, Al-Farabi Kazakh National University is the oldest and largest university in Kazakhstan. It has trained over 100,000 professionals who have made significant contributions to the development of Kazakhstan and other countries. The university was the first in Kazakhstan to receive the Presidential Award for Quality and the CIS Award for excellence in quality and service.

Key achievements include being ranked as the best university in Central Asia, membership in international university associations, and partnerships with over 300 universities worldwide. The university has produced 17 laureates of the State Prize of the Republic of Kazakhstan and numerous internationally recognized researchers.

Leadership & Governance

The university is governed by a rector and academic council, with strong emphasis on international standards in higher education management. The leadership team includes distinguished academics and administrators with extensive experience in higher education and research management.

International Partnerships

KazNU has established partnerships with over 300 universities and research institutions across 36 countries, providing students with extensive opportunities for international exchange and collaboration.

Dual Degree Programs

Students can earn dual degrees through partnerships with universities in the USA, UK, Germany, France, South Korea, and other countries. These programs typically involve 1-2 years of study at partner institutions.

Research Collaboration

Active research collaborations with international institutions in fields such as nanotechnology, renewable energy, biotechnology, and space research. The university participates in numerous international research projects and conferences.
