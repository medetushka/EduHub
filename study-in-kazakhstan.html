Mission & Vision

To be a leading research university that contributes to the sustainable development of Kazakhstan and global society through innovation, education, and research excellence. KazNU aims to be among the top 100 universities worldwide by 2030.

History & Achievements

Founded in 1934, Al-Farabi Kazakh National University is the oldest and largest university in Kazakhstan. It has trained over 100,000 professionals who have made significant contributions to the development of Kazakhstan and other countries. The university was the first in Kazakhstan to receive the Presidential Award for Quality.

Key achievements include being ranked as the best university in Central Asia, membership in international university associations, and partnerships with over 300 universities worldwide.

Campus Facilities

Al-Farabi KazNU offers modern facilities including state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, and student dormitories. The campus is located in the beautiful city of Almaty, surrounded by mountains and offering a vibrant student life.

Student Life

Students can participate in over 50 clubs and organizations, including sports teams, cultural groups, and academic societies. The university hosts regular events, conferences, and cultural festivals that bring together students from around the world.

International Exchange

KazNU has partnerships with 300+ universities worldwide, offering exchange programs including Erasmus+, DAAD, and Fulbright. Students can earn dual degrees through partnerships with universities in the USA, UK, Germany, France, and South Korea.
