Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime? +

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Premium and Business plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For Enterprise plans, we can also arrange custom payment terms.

Can I cancel my subscription? +

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer student discounts? +

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students with valid student ID. Contact our support team to claim your discount.
